# Adams Homes

Most builders pick a lane: community developments or custom scattered lots. Adams does both. Most production builders at this price point nickel-and-dime you with an upgrade menu that bloats the final number. Adams doesn't.

## Choice, Built In

Adams builds in **St. John's Preserve** (building through spring 2027) and scattered single-story homes across Palm Bay and Sebastian. Want community amenities? Pick the development. Want your own lot and more control over location? Pick scattered. Most builders make you choose. Adams doesn't.

## Floor Plans That Actually Fit

They carry enough floor plan variety that if you're looking for it, they probably have it: jack-and-jill bathrooms, dual closets in the primary, three-car garages, open-concept layouts. No "here are three plans, take it or leave it." You know what features matter to your life—Adams has built them.

## Standard Details That Make a Difference

This is where you see 35 years of building. Lever handles on every door. Two-gang light switches throughout. LED lighting standard. Full set of shelves in the pantry *and* linen closets. 16-inch on-center studs. Sloped rooflines with larger eaves for water control and curb appeal. These aren't upgrades. They're standard.

Finish levels vary—carpet and laminate to tile and quartz—but you get honest quality at your price point. No surprises, no hidden costs for the obvious stuff.

## Ready to Close or Build From Scratch

Adams typically has 20+ homes ready for quick close. But if you want to choose your primary bathroom layout or customize finish options, you can build from the ground up. That's the trade: faster close, or more control. You pick.

## What You Customize. What You Don't.

Adams keeps it simple. When you build from scratch, you choose your cabinet colors and carpet or tile selections. You pick between a garden tub and shower or an oversize shower in the primary. That's it. No backsplashes, no ceiling fans, no bloated upgrade menu.

That's not a limitation—it's the philosophy. Keep the base price real. Build the right details in from the start. Don't make buyers pay extra for what they actually need.

35 years in business. Two lot types. One honest approach.
